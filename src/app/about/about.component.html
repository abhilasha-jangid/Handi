Our History

The word handicrafts are made by two words hand + craft. Hand mean by made of hand or using simple tools in making of any item, ad craft mean a unique expression that represent a culture, tradition and the heritage of a country. That mean handicraft is a unique expression by using art on any item. Handicraft is also known as artisanry, which mean a type of work where useful and decorative devices are made completely by hand or using simple tools.

The term handicraft is most frequently used for the 'art and craft". Art and craft work is not only a matter of happy but also creating useful as well as creative work for people, using natural materials and traditional techniques. The history of handicrafts is almost 5000 years old.
